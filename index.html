About Me

Hi, my name is Jarret Yingling. I live in Midtown, Atlanta, GA where I am VP of Capital Markets Trading and Data Analytics for SunTrust Bank's Corporate Treasury. I am currently enrolled in GA Tech's Coding Bootcamp for Full Stack Web Development.

I hope to leverage full stack web development with my knowledge of capital markets trading in Atlanta's burgeoning fintech industry. Capital markets, banking and finance in general are rapidly changing with technological advances on mulitple fronts and I hope to play an active role in this innovation.

I moved to Atlanta several years ago but grew up in central Pennsylvania. I have a voracious appetite for knowledge and enjoy challenging myself to try new things that I've never done before. I'm a self-taught guitar player of 20 years and have recently begun exploring the world of sailing.
